Academic Profile

Dr. Golam Moktader Nayeem is a Computer Science and Engineering academic with 20 years of combined teaching, research and academic leadership experience. He is currently serving as Associate Professor and Acting Dean of the Faculty of Electrical and Computer Engineering at BAIUST. His work connects bio-inspired optimisation, swarm intelligence and reinforcement learning with autonomous UAV and UAV swarm systems. He has led departmental academic development, accreditation preparation and quality assurance activities, while supervising undergraduate thesis and capstone projects.
